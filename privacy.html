Privacy Policy

Rook Rumble — Casual Chess Club • Effective Date: June 29, 2026

1. Data Collection

Rook Rumble — Casual Chess Club ("the App") does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, user information, or analytics.

2. Local Storage

The App runs entirely on your device. All customization settings (such as board themes and piece styles) and game preferences are saved locally in your browser's storage and are never transmitted to any external servers.

3. Multiplayer Functionality

The multiplayer feature operates entirely on your local area network (LAN). The App does not route your gameplay, IP address, or connection data through any global matchmaking servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising SDKs.

5. Changes to This Policy

If the App's data practices change in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer at durbano77@gmail.com.
